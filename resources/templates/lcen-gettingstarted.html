List of required documents

Commercial Loan Requirements
  1. Must have existing checking account with ASO for a minimum duration of 3 months (Individual Mortgage Requirement)
  2. Offer Letter
  3. Valuation report
  4. Pay slips (3 months)
  5. Statement of Account (6 months)

Requirements for Construction and Renovation

  1. Certificate of Occupancy
  2. Approved Building Plan
  3. Original Bill of quantity (stamped, sealed and signed by a quantity surveyor)
  4. Deed of Assignment of sales Agreement
