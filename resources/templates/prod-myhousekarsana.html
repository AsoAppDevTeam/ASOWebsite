ASO Garden Estate is a serene community of exquisite homes on a 27 hectare site, located just after Gwarinpa, along the Kubwa Express Road, precisely beside the papal ground.

The site of the estate is located within the Karsana East District which is part of Abuja Phase IV. The estate will be developed in 5 phases, with the 1st phase consisting of 249 housing units of 2 & 3 bedroom blocks of flat and 3 units of 4 bedroom terrace.

When completed, the estate will comprise of more than 900 homes ranging from 2 & 3 bedroom apartments, 4 bedroom terraces to 5 bedroom fully detached luxury houses. To provide the ultimate lifestyle experience, the estate will have high quality infrastructure, parks, support retail outlets, civic facilities and schools. When complete, the estate will be one of the largest private housing development in Abuja.
