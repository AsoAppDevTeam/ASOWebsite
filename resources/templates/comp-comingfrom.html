Where We Are Coming From

ASO Started out in the beginning as a majorly Government owned company and subsequently evolved to being a Public Limited Liability Company. Today, ASO leads the Mortgage Industry in many parameters through the realization of its strategic objectives.


  • 2008 - Listed on the Nigerian Stock Exchange

  • 2007 - Raised N3.7 billion from private placement

  • 2006 - Inception of new Management Team

  • 2005 - Converted to Public Liability Company (PLC)

  • 2004 - Started Securitization of SOFGH

  • 2002 - Established ASCON (Subsidiary)

  • 2000 - Established ASVEL (Subsidiary)

  • 1997 - Commenced Operations; Established ASIL (Subsidiary)

  • 1995 - Incorporated as a Limited Liability Company

  • 1995 - Licensed as a Primary Mortgage Institution by CBN
