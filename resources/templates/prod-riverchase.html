Features

Riverchase Estate is a serene community of exquisite homes on a 22 hectare site, located just after Gwarinpa, along Kubwa Express Road, right beside Papal ground. The Estate is located within the Karsana East District which is part of Abuja Phase IV. The Estate consists of 1, 2 and 3 Bedroom Apartments, 2 Bedroom Terrace Duplexes and 4 Bedroom Terrace Duplexes.

To provide the ultimate lifestyle experience, the Estate will have high quality infrastructure, parks, support retail outlets, civic facilities and schools. When completed, the Estate will be one of the largest private housing developments in Abuja.
