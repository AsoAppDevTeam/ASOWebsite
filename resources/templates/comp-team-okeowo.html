Amb. Umar Iliya Damagum
Non Executive

Ambassaor Umar Iliya Damagum is a dynamic administrator with experience in board politics spanning a period of 15 years at Goldlink Insurance Plc, Abuja Investment Company Limited, Yobe State Investment and Yobe State Housing Corporation.

He attended several courses, seminars, conferences and workshops in Banking, Economy, Diplomacy and Public Sector Administration. He was further awarded Honorary Diplomas and Certificate by the Governing Board of Adiri, Bucharest- Romania, Iulian Antonescu Museum (Bacau-Romania) and The Family International (Timisoara-Romania

Ambassaor Umar Iliya Damagum was a Council Member of Nigerian Shippers Council from 2002 to 2004 and National Health Insurance Scheme 2012 to 2015. He was the Managing Director/Chairman of Family Investment Nigeria Limited.
