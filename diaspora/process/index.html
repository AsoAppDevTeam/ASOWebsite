Home Buying Process

Here is a description of the Home buying process, what to expect and most importantly what is required of you at each stage of the process from initiation to closing.

10 Steps

Website

Start off on the right foot and visit http://www.asoplc.com/diaspora/ to learn more about the Home@Home Purchase program

Project Listings

Customer selects Projects/Property Listings based on location or pricing

Register

Sign up to access our dedicated Home@Home Customer Care center and to enlist for monthly alerts on property listings

Inquires

Make all inquiries on your Project/Property of interest via dedicated Home@Home Customer Care Center

Offer Letter

Request for an offer letter once you have identified your preferred property to purchase

Legal Representative (Optional)

Select legal (from registered vendors with ASO) to assist with the facilitation of and the home purchase process

Acceptance Of Offer

Review, sign and submit all documents required to conclude on the acceptance of offer. ASO will issue a Provisional Allocation offer once 50% of the total sales prices has been received and an Allocation letter upon receipt of 100% of all payments

Payments

Begin making payments to the Project Account using the Correspondence Banking details

Updates

Receive monthly updates on the progress of your property

Welcome Home

Upon Completion of the Project, ASO sends notifications to handover of keys to all subscribers who have made 100% payments
