Payment Steps

  • For your convenience, payments are made online through your bank account to the designated account for your choice property. ASO’s correspondent bank details are indicated for the receiving account and the property details obtained from the website/portal are specified on your payment instruction.
  • It is compulsory that you quote the unique code on your offer letter.
  • Your payment (s) will be confirmed within one working day after the payment has been received.
