ASO Housing Exhibition - Frequently Asked Questions

Click to expand/collapse

What is the 3rd ASO Housing Exhibition & Conference?

This is a 3 day event organized by ASO, designed to create a forum where all stakeholders involved in the Housing Industry, such as, mortgage providers, Housing corporations, Estate developers. Interior decorators and state Governments etc, come together to showcase their products and services to the Nigerian public. Come join us at the International Conference Centre, Abuja from the 11th through 13th April 2013, 8am - 5pm daily.

I will like to showcase my products and services, how do I make payments?

You go to the website: http://www.asoplc.com/exco to register on line, and afterwards you go to any of our branches to make payments.

As a customer do I need to register to attend the exhibition?

No. There will be registration at the venue.

How will civil servants, traders and small business owners benefit from the Exhibition and Conference?

The exhibition is targeted at different society levels, there are no income limits. Estate developers with affordable houses will be present.

What is the entrance fee?

It's free to the public.

As an expert in the housing industry, what do I benefit from participating in the Exhibition?

The Exhibition enables you to build profitable relationship with Customers and also create awareness for your products and services.
