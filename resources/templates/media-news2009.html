  • MBAN presents Award of Excellence to ASO 26 November, 2009

    • The President of the Mortgage Bankers Association of Nigeria (MBAN) recently presented an award to ASO Savings & Loans PLC The award was received by ASO's MD/CEO Hassan Usman in recognition and appreciation of ASO's laudable support and worthy contribution to the success of MBAN's 9th PMI CEOs' retreat 2009 as platinum sponsor.

      The Award was presented at the MBAN 9th PMI CEOs Annual Retreat/International Finance Corporation (IFC) collaborative workshop held at the Transcorp Hilton Hotel Abuja on November 22, 2009.

  • ASO Savings & Loans PLC grows earnings by 43% 15 November, 2009

    • ASO Savings & Loans PLC has grown its gross earnings by 43%, notwithstanding the impact of the economic meltdown on the financial industry.

      The Primary Mortgage Institution (PMI) declared for the 2009 financial year gross earnings of 10billion, which is an increase by 43% compared to the N7billion it garnered in the previous year.

      The shareholders, at the company's annual general meeting (AGM) in Abuja recently commended the Board and Management of the Bank for the performance, especially for achieving the result in a year many financial institutions are witnessing drop in sales revenue.

  • ASO Sponsored African Master Patrons Cup 2009 9 November, 2009

    • ASO Savings & Loans PLC recently sponsored the prestigious Patrons Cup Polo Championship at the beautiful Fifth Chukker Polo & Country Club Kaduna.

      Top among the guests at the championship were; the Executive Governor of Kaduna State His Excellency Arc. Namadi Sambo, His Royal Highness, the Emir of Zazzau, Alhaji Shehu Idris, the MD/CEO ASO Savings & Loans PLC. Hassan Usman and ASO's top Management team.

  • ASO Wins Microsoft Business Transformation Award May 2009

    • ASO Savings & Loans PLC, a leading mortgage provider wins Microsoft Business Transformation Award 2009.

      ASO Savings & Loans PLC was one of the recognized winners of the Microsoft Business Transformation awards for Enterprise Performance Management - built on Performance Point and SharePoint.

      The Microsoft Business Transformation Award is an annual event and this year's edition was presented to ASO Savings & Loans PLC at a well attended event by the leading banks in Nigeria and blue chip companies held at the Victoria Crown Plaza Hotel Victoria Island, Lagos.
