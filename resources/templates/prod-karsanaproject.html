The Karsana Project is a serene community of exquisite homes on a 22 hectare site, located just after Gwarinpa, along Kubwa Express Road, right beside Papal ground. The Estate is located within the Karsana East District which is part of Abuja Phase IV. The Estate consists of 1, 2 and 3 Bedroom Apartments, 2 Bedroom Terrace Duplexes and 4 Bedroom Terrace Duplexes.
