Home Finance

Overview

This program is specially designed to meet the needs of Nigerians living in Diaspora who wish to own a property or take advantage of real estate investment opportunities in Nigeria.

Explore the ASO Home@Home Program which offers Nigerians in Diaspora a credible, hassle-free and convenient means to home ownership.

There are two payment options:

  1. Outright Purchase Option (one-time payment) — The customer makes a one time full payment for the property.
  2. Installment Plan — The customer makes staggered payments based on the purchase price over a pre-determined period.
