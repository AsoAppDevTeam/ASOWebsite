  • ASO Savings Commissions ASO Life Camp Estate, Life Camp March 16, 2021

    • ASO Savings and Loans Plc has proven to be the leading Primary Mortgage Bank (PMB) in Nigeria over the past several years. The bank has entrenched itself as a leading home provider even in the face of harsh business terrain. This commitment to facilitating homeownership was further proven on Monday, March 15, 2021 when the Bank commissioned Phase 1 of ASO Life Camp Estate, Abuja. The estate boasts spacious living rooms, bedrooms, fitted kitchens with modern accessories, ample parking space, a beautiful landscape.

      The commissioning of the estate was graced with the presence of notable dignitaries which include the Managing Director, Federal Mortgage Bank of Nigeria; Architect Ahmed Musa Dangiwa, and many more.

      The Executive Director, ASO Savings & Loans Plc, Risikatu Ahmed, in her welcome address, noted the impact of the unforeseen COVID-19 pandemic on real estate development. The economic uncertainty caused by the global pandemic has led to homeowners struggling to meet their mortgage repayments and many reconsidering their housing needs.

      She further stated that ASO as a foremost Mortgage Bank has reviewed its business strategy and has developed innovative building and financing solutions to meet the housing needs of prospective homeowners.

      The Bank has also commenced the conceptualization stage of the second phase of the life camp project.
