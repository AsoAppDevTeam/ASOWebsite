MyHouse Purchase Program

The ASO MyHouse Purchase Program is a new program that provides you with an accessible option of purchasing a home. With a structured payment plan, ASO MyHouse Purchase Program enables you to acquire a property by making down payments over the term of the agreement. The customer has two purchase options:

  • Mortgage Option: The Customer makes staggered payments based on his/her equity contribution

  • Outright Purchase Option: The customer makes staggered payments based on the purchase price. Customers who select the Outright purchase option are eligible for a discount off the purchase price

The ASO MyHouse Purchase Program is available on an exclusive range of ASO Real Estate projects. Any sale of properties under the ASO MyHouse Purchase Program is carried out following a strict prequalification.
