ASO Meadows Estate is a planned gated community offering affordable quality houses. The Estate is situated in Kuje district, Abuja and is 20 minutes from the Local and International Airports, 45 minutes from the city centre, and only 15 minutes to Gwagwalada, allowing a quick and easy access to many destinations, in and out of Abuja.

The estate will be expanded in two phases which will be situated on almost 15 hectares and comprise of 338 homes of two-bedroom terrace and two-bedroom semi-detached bungalows. The phase 2 consists of 491 units in total and 120 units would be sold as serviced plots, while 371 units would be sold as partially completed housing units.
